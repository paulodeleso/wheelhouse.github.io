Vito Anzelmi
Director
As co-founder of Wheelhouse Travel Alliance and Director of the company, Vito is actively involved with all WTA clients. Vito Anzelmi is a dynamic leader with over 20 years of international travel and tourism experience and has a proven track record of bringing big ideas to life while maintaining focus, strategic orientation, and ability to lead sales teams.

Formerly Vito was Vice President, Sales - Americas, UK & Europe for Virgin Australia Airlines based in Los Angeles. This role was responsible for the development of sales business plans in line with the Virgin Australia Group long-haul strategies, delivering revenue growth in USA through OTA, TMC, Cruise, retail and tour operator accounts, and driving sales and co-op marketing plans for the market. Vito managed a sales team of 7 staff and implemented CRM for contract management and sales pipeline opportunities.

Previously, Vito was Tourism Australia’s Regional Manager for New Zealand based in Auckland where he was responsible for creation and execution of Tourism Australia’s consumer and trade engagement strategy for the market. He managed consumer and distribution marketing campaigns including digital and print initiatives tied to strategic partners to facilitate sales to Australia.

Vito has also extensive experience in the USA MICE Market, having served as Tourism Australia’s Business Events Manager where he drove the destination’s key incentive house and corporate end user relationships.

Vito holds a Bachelor of Arts in Tourism Management from The Royal Melbourne Institute of Technology, Melbourne Australia (1995).
